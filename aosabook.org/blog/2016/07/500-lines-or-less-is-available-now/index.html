500 Lines or Less is Available Now

Michael DiBernardo - Tue 12 July 2016 -

We're happy to announce that "500 Lines or Less" is now generally available! You can read the web version for free on aosabook.org, while the PDF and paperback versions are available for purchase from Lulu. Within a few weeks, the paperback will also be available on Amazon. All proceeds from sales will go to Amnesty International.

You can read more about the release in our previous post.

Enjoy! We'll see you again in volume 5.
